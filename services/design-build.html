Complete Design-Build Solutions

U.S. Mechanical offers comprehensive design-build services that streamline the entire project process. From initial concept and design through final construction and commissioning, we provide single-source responsibility for all mechanical systems.

Our Design-Build Services Include:

  • Conceptual design and feasibility studies
  • 3D modeling and BIM coordination
  • Value engineering and cost optimization
  • Integrated design and construction
  • Project management and coordination
  • System commissioning and testing

Why Choose Design-Build?

Faster Delivery

Integrated design and construction reduces project timeline and accelerates delivery.

Cost Savings

Single Point of Contact

Optimized Systems
