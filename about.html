About U.S. Mechanical

A History of Plumbing & Mechanical Contracting Expertise. Quality, Service & Safety Since 1963.

Our Story

U.S. Mechanical is a mechanical contractor specializing in the design and build of commercial and industrial hydronic, HVAC, and other process piping systems. From complete design and engineering including the BIM/3D/2D coordination, to the installation of complete mechanical systems, we are focused on quality, service, and safety.

Since 1963, we have built our reputation on delivering exceptional mechanical contracting solutions with a commitment to safety as our top priority. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional mechanical contracting services that exceed client expectations.

Today, we continue to lead the industry with cutting-edge technology, sustainable practices, and a team of highly skilled professionals who share our commitment to excellence in plumbing and mechanical contracting.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional mechanical contracting solutions that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and professionalism.

Our Vision

To be the leading mechanical contractor in the Intermountain West, recognized for innovation, reliability, and commitment to sustainable building practices.

Our Values

Safety first, quality excellence, integrity in all relationships, continuous improvement, and respect for our team, clients, and community.
